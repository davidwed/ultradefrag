UltraDefrag это дефрагментатор дисков с открытым исходным кодом. Может быстро обработать любые файлы, включая те, которые обычно заблокированы Windows, такие как файл подкачки.

НОВОСТИ

UltraDefrag 7.0.0 RC1 has been released [2015-12-20]

We are proud to present you the first candidate for the final UltraDefrag v7 release. In the latest beta we have fixed 99% of application crashes. We'll do our best to improve reliability even further, but taking into account the fact that the remaining crashes are probably caused by hardware failures and virus infections (rather than UltraDefrag bugs), we consider RC1 to be safe for daily use. If you'll find, however, that it still fails for you, don't hesitate to drop us a note. And finally, if you believe we're making good progress, vote for us at SourceForge Project of the Month poll. We strongly believe we're making our world a little bit better together, as we're spreading freedom via free software. Thank you.
