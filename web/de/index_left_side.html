UltraDefrag ist ein leistungsstarkes Open Source Defragmentierungswerkzeug für Windows. Es kann jegliche Systemdateien defragmentieren, einschließlich Registrierungs- und Auslagerungsdateien.

NEUIGKEITEN

UltraDefrag 7.0.0 has been finally released [2015-12-23]

We are proud to present you the final UltraDefrag v7 release. Our tests show it works reliably, so we have no reason to defer it any longer. We'd like to thank all the fans for being with us on the way to the final release. Together we made it and now it's time to think about new features. Some of them should definitely appear in the upcoming 7.1.0 release.
